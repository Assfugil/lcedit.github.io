Livecountsedit

Livecountsedit is a 'customizable (or fake) live subscriber counter' that was created by RandomPerson3465 on July 27, 2019.

Fake live subscriber counters started on January 22, 2019 when RandomPerson3465 uploaded a video of T-Series losing subscribers onto his now deleted YouTube channel. The video received over 600 thousand views by April 2019.

Livecountsedit was created when a Discord user 'Sync' messaged RandomPerson3465 about having a way to edit the subscriber counter on Livecounts.net.

Fun fact: The '3465' in 'RandomPerson3465' comes from the Discord tag of his account at the time he registered an account on Discord. The 'RandomPerson' part is because he is not very creative with usernames.
